Common questions

How do I sign in?
BowPress supports Sign in with Apple, Google, and email. Choose any — you can add others to the same account later in Settings.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?
Deleting the app removes local caches. Your configuration history and session data remain in your BowPress account until you delete it from Settings → Account → Delete Account.

How do I cancel a subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Open the App Store app, tap your profile picture, tap Subscriptions, and manage from there.

I found a bug or have a feature request.
Send details to the email above. Screenshots, steps to reproduce, and your device + iOS version help enormously.
